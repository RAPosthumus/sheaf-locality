# References to include

1. De Combinatoriek van de Bruijn.
   User-supplied PDF:
   https://scispace.com/pdf/de-combinatoriek-van-de-bruijn-4fxzbtgye6.pdf

2. T. van Aardenne-Ehrenfest and N. G. de Bruijn.
   Circuits and trees in oriented linear graphs.
   Simon Stevin, 1951.
   Source of the de Bruijn - van Aardenne-Ehrenfest part of the BEST theorem.

3. BEST theorem.
   De Bruijn - van Aardenne-Ehrenfest - Smith - Tutte theorem.

4. Classical number of de Bruijn sequences:
   \[
   N(q,n)=\frac{(q!)^{q^{n-1}}}{q^n}.
   \]

5. T. Etzion.
   An algorithm for constructing m-ary de Bruijn sequences.

6. Successor-rule frameworks for de Bruijn sequence construction, including PCR/CCR and necklace/co-necklace cycle joining.
